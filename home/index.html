What We Do?

The rise in the usage of internet and dependencies of business ecosystem on cyber world, has increased the dimensions of cyber attacks. We deliever various advance level of Information security training. We provide ondemand information security awareness or training workshops/seminars to the organisations.

We provide support to clients on network penetration testing, Web application penetration testing, and security audits of whole IT department of organisation.
